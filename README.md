# shopnow-python-analisis

Exploratory analisis and cleaning of a dataset from a store which have a focus in tecnology

This is a exercise where we use a dataset from a store to create insights based in a exploratory analisis.

 Data verification
 1.- we didn't find any null value in the "OrderID", "CustomerID" and "OrderDate" columns
 2.- we didn't find any "OrderID" duplicated
 3.- the value in the column "TotalAmount" matches the result given by multiplying the columns "Quantity" and "UnitPrice"

 Ejecutive summary
 1.-Total sales: 9082$
 2.-Total orders: 15
 3.-unique clients: 13
 4.-average client ticket: 698.61$

Recommendations and Insights
January had the most sales, it could be by seasonal holidays or promotional campaigns
Laptops and smartphones represent more of 90% of the sales, we suggest focusing marketing on those categories
Also north and south regions represent 67.9% of the sales, we suggest as well focusing a marketing campaign for those regions

Conclusions
The analisis we did show that the company had a good performance selling laptops and smartphones, with their clients like to pay with credit/debit card, with higher concentration of sales between the north and south regions(which can be good for expansion and grow in those regions)we recommend:
1.-focus in the marketing for the south and north regions
2.-create loyalty campaigns for loyal clients
